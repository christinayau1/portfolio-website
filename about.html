About Christina

My Story

I am currently based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA. ​ When I was 12 years old, my dad first sat me down with a blank piece of paper and pen and told me to think of any problem I could think of and figure out why it did not work and possible solutions for it. Though I did not know or appreciate it at the time, my dad was the first one who encouraged me to think critically and creatively about the world and how I could contribute my ideas to better others' experiences in the world. ​ It took a while for me to join the User Experience (UX) Design field though.  ​ I was a psychology major,  foreign exchange student in China and came back to the U.S. wanting to be a licensed Marriage & Family Therapist (MFT) in China. But sadly, I realized there weren't many MFT opportunities in China after further investigation and came back to the drawing board of figuring out what I wanted to do career-wise. During the pandemic, I took an "Introduction to Design" course on LinkedIn, liked it and looked into how I could do this as a career. Long story short, I heard about UX Design and enrolled in April 2021 at CareerFoundry's User Experience Design program as a full-time student. As a student, I created a payment app called Basil and a vocabulary app called WordBoost. I most enjoyed the user research and user testing component of the design process.  ​ In my free time, I mentor international students, enjoy exploring the outdoors, and love eating fruits (aka I'm a fruit monster).
