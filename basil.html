My Design Process

Research

I conducted three user interviews to see how Basil could better meet user goals and needs and address the problems they experienced with payment apps.

Some key findings from the user interviews were that users experienced frustration when having to pay to instantly transfer their app balance to their bank. Another finding was that users typically used payment apps to split the bill.

Ideation

I decided to not include those in the Boomer Generation and older because using payment applications was not their main payment method. My main target audience were Millennials and Generation Z as most of my users would be making some income and user payment apps regularly in their social circles. Meet Claudia, the Millennial who uses her payment app mainly to split the bill with friends when they go out to restaurants and bars. Meet Bailey, the Generation Z College Student who wants instant transactions and social connection with their friends.

Combining user interview research data and my personal experience, I created user flows based on the three goals the user persona would want to accomplish to visualize their navigation process. Here is one example of the user flow I created for Claudia to add a credit card to her wallet.

Wireframes

I created wireframes and applied them to the Balsamiq application to better visualize what my design would look like on a mobile screen.

Using Adobe XD, I created a more robust mid-fidelity prototype and conducted usability tests. From my usability tests, I learned that most users did not know what my feature "Group Pay" was. I decided to incorporate the functionality of that feature into two main features, "Send" and "Receive", to reduce cognitive load for users instead of having them learn what "Group Pay" was.

Design

One UI lesson I learned about branding was how the initial background image of your app should reflect how you want the user to feel. In this case, rather than having a image of a phone and credit card, I replaced the image with an image of people enjoying life to elicit the kinds of experience and emotions they can have more of because of the ease of taking care of their financial needs using this app.
